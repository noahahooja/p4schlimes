What are Stocks?

A stock, also known as equity is a security that represents the ownership of a certain fraction of the company. Units of stock are called "shares." This entitles the owner of the stock to a proportion of the corporation's assets and profits equal to how much stock they own. Down below is a example stocks graph where you can see how well the companies stocks are doing. You can also use the search bar where the AAPL is to search for different companies by using their stock symbol. Some of the biggest companies on the market are Amazon(AMZ), Apple(AAPL), and Tesla (TSLA).
